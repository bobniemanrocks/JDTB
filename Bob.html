Bob Nieman

Bob has been playing guitar for a long time and while he's got a definite Blues and Rockabilly slant, he enjoys playing the variety of genre's performed by Joanne's Driving The Bus and infusing that spirit into the band's take on both covers and original tunes.

When not "On The Bus" you can find him at one of many local blues jams or open mic nights cranking it out with other musicians.

Check out his own covers and original songs on Spotify or other streaming services or his own site at https://bobnieman.rocks
